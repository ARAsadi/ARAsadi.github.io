I am a Leverhulme Early Career Fellow in the Statistical Laboratory, part of the Department of Pure Mathematics and Mathematical Statistics, at the University of Cambridge. I am also an Isaac Newton Trust Fellow and a Postdoctoral Affiliate of Trinity College. I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Princeton University in 2021. Before that, I received B.Sc. degrees in Mathematics and in Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology, both in 2015. My research interests are statistical machine learning, differential privacy, information theory, and related topics.
